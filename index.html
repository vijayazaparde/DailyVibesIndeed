Google Interview Preparation Tips

  • Practice Google-tagged questions on LeetCode, focusing on the top 100.
  • Aim to complete at least 60-70% of the Google card on LeetCode.
  • Work on topics like dynamic programming, graphs, trees, and heaps.
  • Utilize resources like Striverâ€™s SDE sheet and Fraz Sheet for structured learning.
  • Participate in LeetCode contests to assess your progress.
  • Time yourself: 10 mins for easy, 20 mins for medium, 30 mins for hard problems.
  • Clarify questions within the first 5 minutes during interviews.
  • Communicate your thought process clearly and effectively.
  • Prepare questions to ask interviewers at the end of the interview.
  • Consider mock interviews on platforms like Pramp.
